Overview

Purpose

Have you ever wondered what day of the week your favorite team plays? My page goal is quickly communicating the sports news, a page to see the schedules, results, and resumes of the games. I am a web developer and I love sports; I would like to be updated with the different teams I follow. Many people found this tool efficient and fun. Soon we are going to have a meeting with people who want to be part of this page, would you like to join us in the meeting?

Audience

My Audience is people of all ages, is people interested to be updated in sports all the time, is people that take the time on their phones, computer, and other devices to review the resumes of the game, the results, the news, and the schedule to future games, there are persons that also want to know the opinion of the other people about the game and they like to share their opinions too, this site is going to be a great tool for business people that is so busy and they can’t see the games, because just with a click they will be able to find what is looking for
